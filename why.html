Why Buy a Raffle Ticket?

Yeshivat Torat Yosef is more than a school — it is a cornerstone of our Sephardic community here in Florida, rooted deeply in the values and traditions that shaped our families in Flatbush and beyond.

By purchasing a raffle ticket, you join a community effort to sustain and grow Torah learning, inspire the next generation, and support the unique Sephardic heritage that our yeshiva proudly upholds.

Here’s why your participation matters:

  • Empowering Our Youth: Every ticket helps provide quality Torah education, empowering our children to thrive spiritually, academically, and socially.
  • Preserving Sephardic Tradition: Your support keeps alive the rich customs, melodies, and teachings of our community for generations to come.
  • Strengthening Community Bonds: The raffle brings together families and supporters dedicated to a common mission of growth and unity.
  • Chance to Win Amazing Prizes: One winner will take home a fantastic prize of their choice — all while contributing to a meaningful cause.
  • Transparent and Trustworthy: Yeshivat Torat Yosef operates with integrity and dedication to its mission; your contribution goes directly to the students and programs.

At just $126 per ticket, you get a chance to win incredible prizes and help ensure that our yeshiva continues to shine as a beacon of Torah and Sephardic pride in Florida.

Join us in supporting this vital cause. Together, we build a stronger, more vibrant future for our community — one ticket at a time.
